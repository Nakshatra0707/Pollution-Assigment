Main Causes of Pollution:

1. Fossil Fuels

Lots and lots of fossil fuels are burnt daily. These fossil fuels such as coal, oil and gasoline emit loads of toxic pollutants.


2. Harmful Items

Another big cause of pollution is items such as cigarettes which tend to damage the lungs of the user and pollute the surrounding air.


3. Vehicles

We all have vehicles and they all pollute the air as they burn gasoline and releases a lot of toxic gases.


4. Burning of Garbage

Delhi generates a whopping 9500 tons of waste every day and a lot of it is burned which releases a lot of toxic gases in the air.


5. Agricultural Activities

Pestisides and fertilizers are really harmful for the air.
